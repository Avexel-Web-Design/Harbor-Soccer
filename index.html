Our Programs

Recreational Soccer

Birth Years: 2018-2021

Rec Soccer is a fun, developmental program focused on building skills and learning through game-style play. Perfect for kids who are excited or just curious about soccer.

  • Fall and Spring season
  • Registration will be available in July
  • Cleats and Shin Guards Required
  • No Travel

Travel Soccer

Birth Years: 2011-2017

Travel Soccer is a player development program designed to provide a competitive soccer experience in a supportive, recreational setting.

  • Fall and Spring seasons
  • Practices twice weekly
  • Girls games on Saturdays
  • Boys games on Sundays
  • Cleats and Shin Guards required
  • Travel within Northern Michigan

Learn more about who we play on the Northern Michigan Alliance website.

About Harbor Soccer, Inc.

Our Mission

The mission of Harbor Soccer, Inc. is to promote the game of soccer and enhance each child's skill development. Harbor Soccer, Inc. will promote fair play in a fun, safe and recreational environment while encouraging the development of camaraderie, encouragement of teamwork, and nurturing the skills of each individual player.

Harbor Soccer, Inc. is the governing board, which serves to organize and oversee soccer teams in the Harbor Springs area.

Support Harbor Soccer

Each season, Harbor Soccer serves hundreds of young athletes in our community. The costs of maintaining our fields, providing equipment, hiring referees, and ensuring access to safe facilities are significant. The impact from your support in helping our players is immeasurable.

Your support makes a difference. Each donation helps us continue offering a fun, safe, and engaging soccer experience for kids in the Harbor Springs area. Thank you for being a supporter of our community.

Become a Referee

There is currently a shortage of referees in youth soccer across Michigan, and Harbor Soccer is feeling this impact. We need dedicated individuals who are passionate about the game and want to give back to the community by officiating our matches.

Refereeing is a great way to stay involved in soccer, earn some extra income, and help ensure that our young athletes have quality officiating for their games.

Get Started Today

Visit the Michigan State Referee Association to learn more about certification requirements and begin your journey as a youth soccer referee.
