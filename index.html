Team Information

About Our Soccer Program

Our soccer program runs year-round with two main seasons. The Spring Season runs from April through June, featuring both recreational and travel teams. The Fall Season runs from August through October, continuing our commitment to player development and competitive play.

During the spring, we focus on skill development, team building, and competitive play. Teams practice during weekdays and play games on weekends. Travel teams compete against other Northern Michigan communities, while recreational teams focus on local development and fun.

All players must have proper equipment including cleats and shin guards. Jerseys and other team gear are provided by Harbor Soccer, Inc.

About Harbor Soccer, Inc.

Our Mission

The mission of Harbor Soccer, Inc. is to promote the game of soccer and enhance each child's skill development. Harbor Soccer, Inc. will promote fair play in a fun, safe and recreational environment while encouraging the development of camaraderie, encouragement of teamwork, and nurturing the skills of each individual player.

Harbor Soccer, Inc. (HSI) is the governing board, which serves to organize and oversee soccer teams in the Harbor Springs area. We are proud members of the Northern Michigan Soccer Alliance (NMSA) and Michigan State Youth Soccer Association (MSYSA).
